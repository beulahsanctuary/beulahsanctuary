📖 About Beulah Sanctuary

Beulah Sanctuary is a Christ-centered church committed to:

Worshiping God in spirit and truth

Teaching the Word of God

Serving our community with love

Discipling believers for spiritual growth

Creating a welcoming and prayerful environment for all

Our mission is to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ and build a strong, faith-filled community.
